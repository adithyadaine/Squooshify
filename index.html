About Squooshify

Squooshify is a developer tool designed to streamline the creation of Chrome Extension icons.

Simply upload a high-resolution image, and we'll instantly generate the required 16x16, 48x48, and 128x128 sizes, ready for your manifest file.

×

Privacy Policy

Squooshify runs entirely in your browser.

No images are ever uploaded to a server. All processing happens locally on your device using the Canvas API. Your datas privacy is guaranteed by design.
